    Your Privacy is Protected

    We use the k-Anonymity method to check passwords. Your actual password is never sent to any server. Only the first 5 characters of the SHA-1 hash are transmitted, ensuring complete privacy.

    How it works: Your password is hashed locally in your browser, and only a partial hash is sent to the Have I Been Pwned API to check against known breaches.
